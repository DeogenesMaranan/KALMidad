Your safety and voice matter.
KALMidad is your partner in resilience, a platform designed to empower communities by collecting and sharing vital information during calamities. Our mission is to create a space where you can document the impacts of disasters on your life, whether it's the loss of property, access to resources, or critical infrastructure damage.

WHAT WE DO?

KALMidad is a calamity report collector designed to document and visualize the impacts of natural and man-made disasters. We provide users with an intuitive platform to:

  • Submit Reports: Share detailed accounts of destruction, including photos and descriptions of the damage.
  • Visualize Data: Aggregate reports to highlight the most severely affected areas.
  • Connect with Help: Ensure that relief agencies receive accurate and timely information to prioritize aid delivery.

WHY KALMidad ?

Disasters can leave people feeling isolated and unheard. KALMidad ensures every story matters by turning your reports into actionable insights for first responders, relief organizations, and community planners. Together, we can minimize the impact of disasters and work toward recovery.

Join Us in Making a Difference

By using KALMidad , you're not only sharing your story but also contributing to a safer, more prepared world. Join us in our journey to strengthen disaster resilience and recovery efforts for everyone.

Let's stay strong, stay connected, and rebuild together.
